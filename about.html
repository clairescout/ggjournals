We are sisters who see a lot of people in real need around us. Being poor students ourselves, we decided to try to change the world - one handmade journal at a time. All proceeds go to local individuals and families who need a boost. Your journal will be a treasure for the rest of your life (not to mention your posterity's), and a real blessing to somebody else's.
